About

ALL ABOUT ATEEZ

ATEEZ

ATEEZ(Hangul: 에이티즈) is a South Korean boy band under KQ Entertainment.
The group consisting of members Hongjoong, Seonghwa, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung and Jongho.
They debuted on October 24th, 2018 with Treasure Ep.1: All to Zero

  • Debut: 24 Oct 2018
  • Origin: South Korea
  • Genres: K-pop.hip-hop.trap.EDM
  • Labels: KQ Entertainment
  • Members:

    Hongjoong.Seonghwa.Yunho.Yeosang.San.Mingi.Wooyoung.Jongho

ATEEZ is an acronym for "A TEEnager Z", It also stands for "A to Z" meaning everyone, they are a group of teenagers who are aiming to do everything they can enthusiastically.
